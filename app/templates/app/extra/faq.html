Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping at DripSpace

We offer multiple shipping options to suit your needs. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free on orders over $75. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $15, and next-day delivery is $25. International shipping is available to select countries with delivery times of 7-14 business days.

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unworn items with original tags attached. Returns are free for US customers via our prepaid return label. Exchanges are processed immediately upon receipt. Sale items can be returned for store credit only. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in both inches and centimeters. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart for the best fit. Our customer reviews also include fit feedback from verified purchasers. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a relaxed fit or sizing down for a more fitted look.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer Afterpay and Klarna for buy now, pay later options on orders over $35. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your protection.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by clicking the link in the email or logging into your DripSpace account and viewing your order history. Tracking information is typically updated within 24 hours of shipment.

Yes! We offer a 15% discount for verified students and active military members. Verify your status through our partner SheerID at checkout. Once verified, the discount will be automatically applied to your current order and saved to your account for future purchases.

Each garment includes a care label with specific instructions. Generally, we recommend washing in cold water, turning items inside out, and avoiding bleach. Hang dry when possible to maintain fabric quality and shape. For premium items like wool or silk, dry cleaning is recommended. Proper care ensures your DripSpace pieces last for years.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After this window, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you need to make changes. Once shipped, you can refuse the package or initiate a return upon receipt.
